Why Footsteps?

We wanted more genuine and varied travel advice for visitors in Stockholm by crowdsourcing from the community. It will showcase lesser-known areas and bring like-minded people with specific interests together. Additionally, your friend can also design custom travel tips for you!

With 10 000 000 visitors and 40 000 new residents moving in to Stockholm every year, Reddit and other platforms have generated a great number of threads discussing itineraries in Stockholm. Even for Stockholmers, it gets old walking down the same streets and visiting the usual cafes or restaurants.

Now, how about experiencing the city from another persons perspective, and walk a day in their footsteps? Or show the way?

How does it work?

  1. Start by plotting your favorite walking route on the map, edit the route by dragging any point of the line.
  2. Write a short description of your route and save it. This adds your route to our database and allows others to view it!
  3. Check out all the saved routes by clicking the "See routes" tab.
